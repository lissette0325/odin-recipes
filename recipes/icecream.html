Strawberry Ice Cream

Three scoops of strawberry ice cream in a white bowl.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Milk and cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Food coloring

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Combine the strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.
  3. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker.
  4. Freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
  5. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
