Sugar Cookies

Sugar cookies in different colors and different shapes.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Cover and chill the dough.
